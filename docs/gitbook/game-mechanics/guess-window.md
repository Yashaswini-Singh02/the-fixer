# The Guess Window

Getting fixed isn't the end of the story. When a fix **lands on you**, the game opens a **guess window** right after the reveal — your chance to call out who's been sabotaging you.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/guess-window.png" alt="The guess window prompting a fixed player to name their fixer"><figcaption><p>A fix landed on you — name the culprit before the window closes.</p></figcaption></figure>

## How it works

* The window opens **only if a fix landed on you** this segment.
* You have **30 seconds** to name your fixer(s) from the other players.
* You name **as many people as fixed you** — one name if one fix landed, more if you were dogpiled.
* Guess right and you bank a **+2 coin bonus** next segment (10 → 12).

## The "miss at most one" rule

You don't have to be perfect. You clear the guess as long as you **miss at most one** fixer:

<table><thead><tr><th width="220">Fixes that landed on you</th><th width="180">Correct names needed</th><th>In words</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>1</td><td>1</td><td>Name your one fixer.</td></tr><tr><td>2</td><td>1</td><td>Get at least one of the two.</td></tr><tr><td>3</td><td>2</td><td>Get at least two of the three.</td></tr><tr><td>4</td><td>3</td><td>Get at least three of the four.</td></tr></tbody></table>

The more people gang up on you, the more forgiving the threshold — but you still have to read the room.

{% hint style="info" %}
The guess window **pauses the game**. The next segment doesn't open until every fixed player has guessed or the 30-second window closes, so nobody misses their chance to retaliate.
{% endhint %}

## Why it matters

The guess window keeps fixing from being a one-way street. A fixer who lands a job stays anonymous to the room — **but not necessarily to their victim.** If you can feel who's been targeting you and name them, you claw back a bonus and confirm your suspicion, even though the rest of the table still won't know until the confessions at full time.

Bonuses **stack**: a player who lands a fix _and_ correctly guesses their own fixer in the same reveal collects both — 10 + 4 + 2 = **16 coins** next segment. See the full breakdown in **[Coins & Bonuses](coins.md)**.
