# Coins & Bonuses

Coins are **ammo, not score**. You get a fresh batch every segment, you spend them on bets and fixes, and whatever you don't spend **disappears**. They never carry over on their own — only the bonuses and penalties below change how many you start a segment with.

## The base allowance

Every player starts every segment with **10 coins**.

Then, based on what happened last segment, the number is adjusted **in this order**:

1. **Halve it** if your fix backfired last segment (10 → 5).
2. **Add bonuses** on top — a landed fix and/or a correct guess.

Because the halving happens first and bonuses add after, they **compose** cleanly.

## The full ledger

{% hint style="info" %}
**Fix-locked** means you can't place a fix that segment at all — a one-segment timeout on top of the halved coins. It only comes from a backfire.
{% endhint %}

## What coins buy

<table><thead><tr><th width="160">Action</th><th width="120">Cost</th><th>Notes</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Place a bet</td><td>1–10 coins</td><td>Your stake. Bigger stakes climb more when they win.</td></tr><tr><td>Fix a friend</td><td>2 coins</td><td>Secret. Lands or backfires at the reveal.</td></tr></tbody></table>

{% hint style="warning" %}
There's **no banking**. Unspent coins vanish at the next segment open, so there's no reason to sit on them — spread them across markets or commit to a fix.
{% endhint %}

The room shows you a **preview** of your next-segment allowance right in the reveal (e.g. _14 · fix-locked_), so you always know what you're walking into.
