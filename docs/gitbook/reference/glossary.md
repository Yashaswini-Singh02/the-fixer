# Glossary

<table><thead><tr><th width="200">Term</th><th>Meaning</th></tr></thead><tbody>
<tr><td><strong>Allowance</strong></td><td>The coins you're granted at the start of a segment. Base is <strong>10</strong>, adjusted by bonuses and penalties. See <a href="../game-mechanics/coins.md">Coins &#38; Bonuses</a>.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Backfire</strong></td><td>What happens to a fix when the target wins something that segment: no rungs, the fixer is named on the spot, and next segment they're fix-locked with a halved allowance.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Bet</strong></td><td>A sealed YES/NO wager on one of the three markets, staked with coins.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>CARD</strong></td><td>The 🟨 market: resolves YES if <strong>1 or more cards</strong> (yellow or red) are shown during the segment.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Climb / Rung gain</strong></td><td>Moving up the ladder. Comes from winning bets and landed fixes.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Coins</strong></td><td>Per-segment ammo for bets and fixes. Unspent coins vanish at each segment open — they don't carry over.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Confessions</strong></td><td>The full-time list that finally names every fix that <em>landed</em> during the game.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>CORNERS</strong></td><td>The 🚩 market: resolves YES if <strong>2 or more corners</strong> are awarded during the segment.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Fix</strong></td><td>A secret 2-coin bet that a chosen friend will win nothing this segment. Lands (anonymous rungs) or backfires (named, penalized).</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Fix-locked</strong></td><td>A one-segment ban on placing fixes, applied after your fix backfires.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Fixer</strong></td><td>The player who placed a fix. Anonymous until full time if the fix lands.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>GOAL</strong></td><td>The ⚽ market: resolves YES if <strong>1 or more goals</strong> are scored during the segment.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Guess window</strong></td><td>A 30-second window that opens for a player a fix landed on, to name their fixer(s) for a coin bonus. See <a href="../game-mechanics/guess-window.md">The Guess Window</a>.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Host / Organizer</strong></td><td>The first player to join a room. The only one who can press <strong>Kick off</strong>.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Kick off</strong></td><td>The host action that starts the game and opens Segment 1.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Ladder</strong></td><td>The shared 20-rung climb that is the game's scoreboard. First to the top, or highest at full time, wins.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Landed fix</strong></td><td>A fix whose target won nothing: the fixer climbs 1–3 rungs anonymously and earns a coin bonus.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Market</strong></td><td>One of the three yes/no questions per segment: GOAL, CORNERS, CARD.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Miss at most one</strong></td><td>The guess-window threshold: you clear the guess as long as you correctly name all but one of your fixers.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Payout</strong></td><td>The odds multiplier on a market side (e.g. 2.5×). Fair odds — no house margin — capped at 6.0×.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Red card</strong></td><td>A real-match event that slides everyone above rung 10 down 1 rung.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Replay</strong></td><td>A finished real match re-run at speed (default 10×) so it can be played any time.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Reveal</strong></td><td>The full-screen takeover at the end of a segment: outcomes land, bets unseal, fixes resolve, the ladder climbs.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Room</strong></td><td>A single game session, joined via a 4-letter code or link. 2–8 players.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Rung</strong></td><td>One step on the 20-step ladder.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Seal / Sealed</strong></td><td>The point when the stake window closes and bets/fixes lock. Sealed bets stay secret until the reveal.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Segment</strong></td><td>A 15-minute block of the match. 1–6 in regulation, 7–8 in extra time.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Stake</strong></td><td>The number of coins put on a bet (1–10). Bigger stakes climb more when they win.</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Stake window</strong></td><td>The 180 match-seconds from a segment's open during which bets and fixes are allowed.</td></tr>
</tbody></table>
