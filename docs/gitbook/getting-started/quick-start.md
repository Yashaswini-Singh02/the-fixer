# Quick Start

You need three things: **a match to watch**, **2–8 people**, and **a phone each**. That's it — no accounts, no downloads.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/quickstart-lobby.png" alt="The lobby with a room code and joined players"><figcaption><p>The lobby: your 4-letter room code up top, friends filling in as they join.</p></figcaption></figure>

## Host a room

1. **Open the app** and pick the match you're all watching. Live games sit under **"Live"**, upcoming ones under **"Coming up"**, and finished games under **"Replay the latest matches."**
2. Tap the match. You get a **4-letter room code** and a shareable link.
3. **Send the link** to your friends (there's a native share button).
4. Set your **name and emoji** when you first land in the room.
5. Wait for everyone to join — you'll see them appear live in the lobby.
6. As the host (the first person in), you get the **Kick off** button. Press it when the match — or the replay — is about to start.

{% hint style="warning" %}
You need **at least 2 players** to kick off. Two browser tabs on the same phone count as _one_ player — use separate phones, or one normal window plus one incognito window, to test with yourself.
{% endhint %}

## Join a room

* **Tap the link** a friend shared, **or**
* Open the app and **type the 4-letter code** into "Join a room."

Then pick your name and emoji, and you're in the lobby waiting for kick-off.

## Your first segment (what to expect)

The moment the host kicks off, **Segment 1** opens and everyone gets **10 coins**. Three market cards appear with live odds. Tap **YES** or **NO** on a card, choose a stake, and you've placed a sealed bet. When the stake window closes the cards stamp **SEALED** — now you just watch the match and wait for the reveal.

New to the flow? Read **[How a Match Flows](match-flow.md)** next.

{% hint style="info" %}
**No sound at first?** The win fanfare only unlocks after your first tap on the screen — that's a browser rule, not a bug. Tap anything once and audio comes alive.
{% endhint %}
