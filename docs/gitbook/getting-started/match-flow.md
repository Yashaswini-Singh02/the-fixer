# How a Match Flows

A game of THE FIXER is the match itself, split into chapters. Each **segment** is one 15-minute chapter, and every segment runs the same four beats.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/match-flow-timeline.png" alt="A timeline showing six 15-minute segments across a match"><figcaption><p>A full match: six regulation segments of 15 minutes each, plus extra-time segments if needed.</p></figcaption></figure>

## The segment loop

<table><thead><tr><th width="60">Beat</th><th width="150">Phase</th><th>What you do</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>1</td><td><strong>Open</strong></td><td>Fresh <strong>10 coins</strong> land. Three markets appear with live odds. Place your sealed bets and (optionally) fix a friend.</td></tr><tr><td>2</td><td><strong>Stake window</strong></td><td>You have a limited window to bet. A countdown runs; the odds are live.</td></tr><tr><td>3</td><td><strong>Sealed</strong></td><td>The window closes. Cards stamp <strong>SEALED</strong>. No more bets or fixes — just watch the match.</td></tr><tr><td>4</td><td><strong>Reveal</strong></td><td>The segment ends. The real match decides the outcomes, bets unseal, the ladder animates, and any fixes resolve.</td></tr></tbody></table>

Then the next segment opens and it starts again. **Unspent coins vanish** at each open — there's no saving up.

## The full arc of a match

* **Segments 1–6** — regulation time, one per 15-minute block (0–15, 15–30, … 75–90).
* **Segments 7–8** — extra time, _only if the match goes there_.
* **Half time / full time** — the match's own phases; the game follows the real whistle, not a fixed clock.

{% hint style="info" %}
Segments follow the **match clock**, not your wall clock. In a 10× replay a 15-minute segment is over in about 90 seconds, and the stake window is only a few seconds — bet fast.
{% endhint %}

## The reveal — the loudest moment

The reveal is the point of the whole game. It plays out in three acts:

1. **Outcomes land.** ⚽ GOAL: YES. 🚩 CORNERS: NO. 🟨 CARD: YES.
2. **Bets unseal.** Everyone's picks flip face-up at once — now you see what your friends actually did.
3. **Fixes resolve.** Sabotage pays off or blows up in someone's face, and the ladder climbs.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/reveal-overlay.png" alt="The full-screen segment reveal"><figcaption><p>The reveal takeover: outcomes, then everyone's unsealed bets, then the fixes.</p></figcaption></figure>

If _you_ climbed, you get the full celebration — confetti, a dancing cat 🐈, a chiptune fanfare, and a floating **"+N RUNGS!"**

## After the whistle

When the match hits full time, the game ends. Whoever is highest on the ladder wins — and the **confessions** screen names every fix that landed all game. See **[Full Time](../game-mechanics/full-time.md)**.
